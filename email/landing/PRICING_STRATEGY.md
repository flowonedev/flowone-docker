# MailFlow Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

MailFlow operates on a **"free core, paid convenience"** model:
- Self-hosting is completely free with all features
- Managed hosting for those who don't want to manage servers
- Business tier for teams and agencies

This approach:
1. Maximizes adoption through open access
2. Builds trust with the technical community
3. Creates upsell path for non-technical users
4. Generates revenue from convenience, not artificial limitations

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1: Self-Hosted (Free Forever)

**Price:** $0

**Target Audience:**
- Developers and tech professionals
- Existing VPS/mail server owners
- Privacy enthusiasts
- Budget-conscious small businesses

**What's Included:**
- All features (no artificial limitations)
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited email accounts
- Email tracking
- AI features (user's OpenAI API key)
- Calendar sync
- Personal drive
- Server-side filters
- All updates

**What's NOT Included:**
- Hosting/infrastructure (user provides)
- Email delivery (user's mail server)
- Technical support (community only)
- Managed updates

**Cost to User:**
- VPS: $5-20/month typical
- Domain: $10-15/year
- Mail server setup time

**Marketing Message:**
"Deploy MailFlow on your server in 5 minutes. All features, no cost, forever."

---

### Tier 2: Managed Hosting ($19/month)

**Price:** $19/month (or $180/year = $15/mo)

**Target Audience:**
- Small business owners
- Freelancers wanting professional email
- Non-technical users
- Users who want "it just works"

**What's Included:**
- Everything in Self-Hosted
- Custom domain (your-company.com)
- 5 email accounts/users
- 25 GB total storage
- Daily automated backups
- SSL/security managed
- Email delivery (we handle DNS/DKIM/SPF)
- Email support (48h response)
- Automatic updates

**What's NOT Included:**
- White-label/custom branding
- Priority support
- Additional storage beyond 25GB
- More than 5 accounts

**Profit Margin:**
- Infrastructure cost: ~$5-8/month
- Support overhead: ~$3/month
- Margin: ~$8-11/month (40-60%)

**Marketing Message:**
"Professional email on your domain. We handle the tech, you handle the business."

---

**Target Audience:**

**Marketing Message:**

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring
- Show Self-Hosted ($0) and Business ($49) to make Managed ($19) look like best value
- "Most Popular" badge on Managed tier

### Value Framing
- Don't compare to Gmail (free) - compare to:
  - Superhuman: $30/seat/month
  - Hey: $99/year
  - Mailtrack Pro: $10/month (just tracking!)
  - Zoho Mail: $4/user/month

### No Per-Seat Pricing
- Major differentiator vs. enterprise options
- Attractive to growing teams
- Simple to understand and budget

### Annual Discount
- 2 months free on annual (16.7% discount)
- Improves cash flow
- Reduces churn

## Pricing FAQ

**Q: Why is self-hosted free?**
A: We believe in open access. The technical community builds trust and spreads the word. Managed hosting pays the bills.

**Q: How does AI cost nothing extra?**
A: You bring your own OpenAI API key. We don't add markup - you pay OpenAI directly at their rates.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my limits?**
A: We'll notify you before any limits. You can upgrade anytime, prorated. We never delete your data.

**Q: Can I switch between tiers?**
A: Yes. Upgrade instantly, downgrade at next billing cycle. Data migration is seamless.

**Q: Is there a money-back guarantee?**
A: 30-day money-back, no questions asked, on all paid tiers.
