## Production Notes

### Visual Style
- Dark theme matching landing page (#18181b background)
- Orange accents for highlights (#f97316)
- Clean, minimal UI animations
- No faces (keeps it universal)
- Focus on product, not stock footage

### Audio
- Calm, confident male or female voice
- Light ambient music (electronic/minimal)
- Subtle UI sound effects
- No cheesy transitions

### Format Specifications
- Main video: 16:9 for YouTube, 1:1 square for social
- Resolution: 4K master, export 1080p
- Captions: Burned in, white text, bottom third

### Distribution
- YouTube (full versions)
- LinkedIn (short versions 30s)
- Twitter/X (15s clips)
- Website (autoplay muted with controls)
- Product Hunt (feature for launch)
