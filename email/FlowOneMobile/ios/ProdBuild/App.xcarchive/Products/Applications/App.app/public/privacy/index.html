Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

FlowOne.Pro is operated by Pixel Ranger Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user data, including data accessed through Google APIs.

1. Information We Process

FlowOne.Pro processes only the data necessary to provide its core functionality. When you sign in with your Google account using Google OAuth, and only after explicit consent, the application accesses:

  • Basic profile information (openid, email, profile) — used to sign you in with Google, create or identify your account, and display your name, email address, and profile picture. This is the only data accessed for standard sign-in.
  • Gmail mailbox data (optional) — only if you choose to connect a Gmail mailbox, via the https://mail.google.com/ scope, so you can read, send, organize, label, search, and manage that email inside FlowOne.Pro.
  • Google Calendar and Google Contacts (optional) — only if you explicitly enable and connect those features, and only for the corresponding scopes you grant at that time.

We do not access any Google user data without your authorization, and we request only the scopes required for the features you use.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Google user data is used solely to provide the features of the application, including:

  • Signing you in and identifying your account, and displaying your name, email address, and profile picture
  • Reading, sending, composing, and replying to your email — only if you connect a Gmail mailbox
  • Organizing connected mailboxes with folders, labels, and server-side filters
  • Searching your messages and displaying conversations and threads for connected mailboxes
  • Calendar and contact features, only where you have connected those optional integrations

We do not use Google user data for advertising, profiling, marketing, or to train AI or machine-learning models.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Google user data is stored only as long as necessary to provide the requested functionality or as required for legitimate operational purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Transfer

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer Google user data to third parties. Google user data is not shared except:

  • When required by law
  • When necessary to provide core service functionality

FlowOne.Pro complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. User Rights and Revocation

Users may:

  • Revoke Google account access at any time via: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Request access to their stored data
  • Request correction or deletion of their data

Data deletion requests can be submitted via email.
