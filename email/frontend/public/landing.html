A self-hosted business email, calendar, drive, chat, and CRM platform you run on your own server. Sign in with your Google account to get started.

Email, Team Chat, CRM, Mood Boards, Drive, Calendar, Boards PRO, Automations, Client Portal — all unified in one platform on infrastructure you own. Multilingual (HU/EN). No per-seat fees. No vendor lock-in.

FlowOne.Pro doesn't replace your tools. It replaces all of them.

What FlowOne.Pro does: FlowOne.Pro is a self-hosted business communication and productivity application for managing email, calendar, files, chat, and client work in one place. You sign in with your Google account using Google OAuth, which lets FlowOne.Pro confirm your identity and show your name, email address, and profile picture inside the app. You can also optionally connect a Gmail mailbox to read and send that email from within FlowOne.Pro. Google user data is used only to provide these features and is never sold or used for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for details.
